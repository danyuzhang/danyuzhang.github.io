About

      I am a PhD candidate from math department at the Ohio State University. My thesis advisor is Andrey Gogolev. I am generally interested in topology and geometry, dynamics related or not.

      Before I came here, I received my MA in math from Binghamton University in 2017 and BSc in math from University of Macau in 2015.

      My name in simplified Chinese: 张丹彧.

 

Research

      I am working on classification problems of hyperbolic dynamics, currently looking at manifolds that could possibly support Anosov diffeomorphisms.
